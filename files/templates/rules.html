Dank Christian Rules

  • No Sexually explicit or suggestive posts. This includes fictitious content. Breaking this rule will result in an instant ban.
  • Links to potentially graphic or upsetting images related to the persecution of the Church are allowed but must be marked as NSFW. Posting gore for the sake of gore is not allowed.
  • No Blasphemous posts or content. Everyone is welcome here regardless of your beliefs (or lack thereof), however we ask that you are respectful and refrain from posting content that would be considered blasphemous.

    • This includes but is not limited to, memes depicting Jesus or God in a disrespectful way, taking the Lord's name in vain, photoshopping Holy Icons, and mocking the Theotokos

  • Excessive post or comment spamming is not allowed, doing so will result in potential strikes, and the spam in question being removed.
  • Self Promotion is allowed, however you must clearly indicate in the post or title of your post that it is self promotion. Excessive posting of self promotion will be treated the same as spamming, which can result in strikes and the content being removed.

    • To avoid self promotion spam, you must contribute more to the community than simply spamming links to your own content.

  • Dank Christian is a place for civil discussion, and as such you may not:

    • Use racial slurs in posts, comments, private messages, or usernames.

    • Harrass other users

    • Advocate for violence against any person, or group of people in real life

    • Evade a user's block by making an alternate account to harrass them.

    Admins reserve the right to reflair you if we deem your flair inaccurate or purpously misleading.
